About OCROWN Veterinary Hospital

Ibadan's trusted veterinary care facility. Providing expert, compassionate healthcare for all your beloved pets.

OCROWN Veterinary Hospital is a modern, fully equipped veterinary facility located in Ibadan, Oyo State. We are dedicated to providing the highest quality medical care for your pets — from routine checkups to complex surgical procedures.

🏅

Certified Excellence

All our veterinarians are fully certified and regularly trained on the latest veterinary practices and techniques.

🔬

Modern Equipment

State-of-the-art diagnostic tools, in-house laboratory, and surgical theatre for comprehensive pet care.

❤️

Compassionate Care

We treat every pet as our own — with patience, love, and expert medical attention from first visit onwards.

🚑

24/7 Emergency

Round-the-clock emergency veterinary services because your pet's health can't wait until morning.

Our Mission

To provide every pet in Ibadan and beyond with the highest standard of veterinary care, delivered with compassion, integrity, and clinical excellence — because your pet deserves nothing less.
